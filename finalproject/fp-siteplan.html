Site Name

Medi-Care Services - Helping you maintain a healthy status till you arrive at your Doctor's

Site Purpose and Goals

At Medi-Care, we are committed to providing relaible, accurate and effective first-aid and medical information, to help people suffering from one ailment or the other learn how to identify the symptoms, what first-aid application to perform, annd connect with the nearest medical professional to ensure they get proper treatment.
We aim to reduce mortality rate by at least 15% every year. We plan to achieve this by also promoting healthy lifestyle and giving health advices to help people maintain good health.

Target Audience

Our goal is to reach everyone, but the priority would be:
  • Bankers.
  • Students
  • Industrial workers.
  • Stay-at-homes.
